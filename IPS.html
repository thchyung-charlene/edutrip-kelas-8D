The Rainbow Village

Our task for IPS (Social Studies) was to conduct an interview about a tourist site from our selected destinations. I chose Rainbow Village because I was intrigued by the history and meaning behind the place itself. Hereâ€™s what I learned from the interview:

Rainbow Village is a vibrant tourist spot and ancestral property. In the past, it served as a rest area for ex-military personnel. Later, it was colorfully decorated by protestors to prevent its demolition. The village is now surrounded by apartments and hotels. It offers opportunities for the younger generation and regularly updates its walls with new illustrations. Those who paint here bring joy not only to themselves but also to everyone who visits.
